About Reina DMV Services

Reina DMV Services was founded with a simple mission: to save you the time, stress, and frustration of dealing with the DMV yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our DMV services.

What DMV services do you offer?

We offer a full range of DMV services including original registrations, re-registrations, duplicate registrations, duplicate titles, plate surrenders, replacement of stolen or lost plates, title-only transactions, 18-wheeler registrations, registration renewals, boat transactions, and amended registrations.

What states do you serve?

We currently serve New York, Florida, New Jersey, and Virginia. If you’re in another state, give us a call — we may be able to assist or point you in the right direction.

Do I need to visit the DMV myself?

No. That’s the whole point of our service. We handle all DMV visits and paperwork on your behalf so you never have to wait in line.

How long does the process take?

Turnaround times vary by service and state, but most transactions are completed within a few business days. Some services like registration renewals can be handled same-day. Call us for a specific estimate.

What documents do I need to provide?

The required documents depend on the specific service. When you call us, we’ll provide you with a clear checklist of exactly what’s needed — no guessing required.

How much do your services cost?

Our fees vary based on the type of service and state requirements. Call us at (516) 554-1144 for a free, upfront quote with no hidden fees.

Do you handle boat registrations and titles?

Yes. We process boat and marine vessel transactions including registrations, titles, and renewals across all four states we serve.

What are your business hours?

We’re available Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM Eastern Time. Give us a call or text anytime during business hours.

Can you help with a vehicle I purchased from a private seller?

Absolutely. We can handle the title transfer and new registration for vehicles purchased through private sales. Just call us and we’ll walk you through the documents you’ll need.

What if my registration or plates are expired?

We can help with that. Whether your registration recently lapsed or has been expired for a while, we’ll process the re-registration and get you back in compliance.
